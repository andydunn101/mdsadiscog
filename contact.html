Listening to Miles Live

Unfortunately for this world Miles has passed away, but if you want to listen to his music live and by the man himself there are many live albums and DVDs that can be viewed.

For more information about Miles read his autobiography "Miles: The Autobiograpy" or go to the offical website https://www.milesdavis.com/

Tribute Acts

There are tribute acts available to see like the Miles Ahead Group in San Fransico. Check them out via their Facebook page https://www.facebook.com/MilesAheadgroup/.
